## Context
Use this prompt when you need to fuse together all communications about a specific topic into a unified briefing with timeline, decisions, and status.

## Prompt
```
Fuse together all emails, attachments, and threads about [TOPIC] from the last [TIMEFRAME]. Produce: 1) Unified timeline of events and decisions, 2) All decisions made with date and decision-maker, 3) Requirements or scope changes over time, 4) Contradictions or conflicting information, 5) Missing information or gaps, 6) Current status and next steps, 7) Key stakeholders and their positions. Provide a 1-page briefing document.
```
