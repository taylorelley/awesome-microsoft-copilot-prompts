## Context
Use this prompt before renewal discussions to compile contract terms, pricing discussions, value realized, objections, competitive mentions, and decision maker information.

## Prompt
```
Search for all discussions related to [Customer Name]'s upcoming renewal. Compile:
- Contract terms mentioned
- Pricing discussions
- Value realized mentions
- Concerns or objections raised
- Competitive mentions
- Decision makers and their positions
- Timeline discussions

This is research for renewal planning. Actual contract details in CRM.
```
