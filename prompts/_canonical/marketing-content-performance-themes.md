## Context
Use this prompt to identify content themes, topics that resonated, content types mentioned positively, and gaps from sales and customer feedback.

## Prompt
```
Review feedback and discussions about our content over the past quarter. Identify:
- Topics that resonated well
- Content types mentioned positively
- Gaps or requests from sales/customers
- Themes to explore or avoid

This is qualitative input for content strategy.

EXPERIMENTAL: Email feedback may not represent actual content performance. Use analytics for quantitative insights.
```
