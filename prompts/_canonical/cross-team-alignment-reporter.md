## Context
Use this prompt when you need to synthesize discussions across multiple teams to identify alignment gaps, conflicting priorities, and collaboration opportunities.

## Prompt
```
Synthesize discussions from meetings with [TEAM A], [TEAM B], and [TEAM C] over the last month. Identify: 1) Shared goals and dependencies, 2) Conflicting priorities or approaches, 3) Gaps in communication or awareness, 4) Opportunities for collaboration, 5) Risks from misalignment, 6) Recommended sync points or forums, 7) Action plan for better alignment. Create an alignment dashboard for leadership.
```
