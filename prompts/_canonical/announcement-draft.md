## Context
Use this prompt when you need to draft an announcement about a change or news for a specific audience.

## Prompt
```
Draft announcement about [news/change] for [audience]. Structure: 1) What's happening, 2) Why it matters, 3) What changes, 4) Next steps, 5) Contact info. Tone: [professional/enthusiastic/serious]
```
