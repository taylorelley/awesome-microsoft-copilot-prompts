## Context
Use this prompt when you need a tactical execution plan based on your recent emails with prioritized actions and escalation warnings.

## Prompt
```
Based on the last 7 days of emails, generate a personal battle plan with: 1) Top 5 priorities ranked by urgency and impact, 2) Emerging threats or issues to watch, 3) Overdue tasks and their consequences, 4) Tasks with unclear ownership, 5) Tasks likely to escalate if not addressed, 6) Recommended action sequence with time estimates, 7) Delegation opportunities. Format as a tactical execution plan with timelines.
```
