## Context
Use this prompt when you need to structure a meeting agenda with clear objectives, time allocations, and decision processes.

## Prompt
```
Meeting agenda for [purpose] with [attendees]. Duration: [time]. Include: 1) Objective, 2) Topics with time allocations, 3) Pre-work, 4) Decision process, 5) Next steps template
```
