## Context
Use this prompt when you need to analyze recurring meetings for effectiveness, time utilization, participation patterns, and optimization recommendations.

## Prompt
```
Analyze the last [4/8/12] recurring meetings for [MEETING NAME]. Provide: 1) Are we achieving meeting objectives?, 2) Time utilization (productive vs. unproductive), 3) Participation patterns (who speaks, who doesn't), 4) Recurring topics that should be resolved, 5) Topics better handled async or offline, 6) Suggested agenda improvements, 7) Recommended frequency and duration, 8) Who really needs to attend vs. optional. Optimize for effectiveness.
```
