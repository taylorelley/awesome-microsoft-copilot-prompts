## Context
Use this prompt when you need to convert meeting notes into a structured action plan with decisions, action items, risks, and follow-up agenda.

## Prompt
```
Convert these meeting notes into a structured action plan: 1) Meeting summary (2-3 sentences), 2) Decisions made with context, 3) Action items table (Task | Owner | Deadline | Dependencies | Status), 4) Open questions requiring follow-up, 5) Risks or blockers identified, 6) Next meeting agenda items. Format for easy distribution to attendees.
```
