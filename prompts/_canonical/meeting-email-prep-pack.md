## Context
Use this prompt when you need to prepare for a meeting by extracting context, agenda items, questions, and talking points from related emails.

## Prompt
```
For the meeting invite + related email thread below, produce:
* Context
* Agenda
* Questions I should ask
* Decisions needed
* Suggested talking points
```
