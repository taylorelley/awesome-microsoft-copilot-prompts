## Context
Use this prompt for content calendar planning to search emails and documents for content ideas, requests, themes, deadlines, formats, and SEO priorities.

## Prompt
```
You are assisting a content creator. Search my emails and documents for content ideas, requests, and plans discussed. Compile:
- Content topics requested by stakeholders
- Themes or campaigns mentioned
- Deadlines discussed
- Content formats suggested
- SEO or keyword priorities mentioned

This is input for content calendar planning.
```
