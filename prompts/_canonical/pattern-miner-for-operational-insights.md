## Context
Use this prompt when you need to identify recurring patterns, blockers, and inefficiencies from email communications for process improvement.

## Prompt
```
Perform pattern mining on all emails received this month. Identify recurring: 1) Types of requests and their frequency, 2) Blockers preventing progress (categorized), 3) Delays and their root causes, 4) Risks mentioned across conversations, 5) Operational inefficiencies or process gaps, 6) Topics consuming most time/attention. Produce a root-cause analysis for top 3 issues and recommended improvements with expected impact.
```
