## Context
Use this prompt when you need a high-level executive summary of the most important items in your inbox from the past week.

## Prompt
```
Create a 10-bullet executive summary of everything important in my inbox from the past week: decisions, approvals needed, deadlines, customer issues, and escalations.
```
