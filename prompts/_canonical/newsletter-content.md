## Context
Use this prompt when you need to write a newsletter section that informs and engages your audience with a clear structure.

## Prompt
```
Newsletter section about [topic] for [audience]. Format: 1) Subject line, 2) Opening (2-3 sentences), 3) Content (3-5 bullets/paragraphs), 4) Action item, 5) Sign-off. Tone: [conversational/professional/enthusiastic]
```
