## Context
Use this prompt to compile onboarding checklists covering IT equipment, access requirements, workspace arrangements, training, and introduction meetings.

```

- IT equipment needs mentioned
- Access requirements discussed
- Workspace arrangements
- Training scheduled
- Introduction meetings
- First week activities

Compare against our standard checklist for completeness.
```
