## Context
Use this prompt when you need to scan your inbox for emails requiring action and get a prioritized list with next steps.

## Prompt
```
Scan the last 14 days of my inbox. List all emails requiring action from me. For each email: summarize the request, identify the sender, propose the next step, and estimate urgency.
```
