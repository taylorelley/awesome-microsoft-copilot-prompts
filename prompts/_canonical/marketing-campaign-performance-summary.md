## Context
Use this prompt for campaign reviews to gather goals, performance feedback, issues, optimization suggestions, and stakeholder reactions from emails and Teams.

## Prompt
```
You are assisting a marketing manager. Search my emails and Teams for discussions about the [Campaign Name] campaign. Summarize:
- Goals and KPIs mentioned
- Performance feedback received
- Issues or challenges raised
- Optimization suggestions discussed
- Stakeholder reactions

This is for my campaign review. Actual metrics come from analytics platforms.
```
