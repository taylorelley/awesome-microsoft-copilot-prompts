## Context
Use this prompt when you need to make a balanced decision by systematically weighing the advantages and disadvantages of an action or choice.

## Prompt
```
Considering: [decision/action]. Comprehensive analysis: 1) Short-term pros/cons, 2) Long-term pros/cons, 3) Risks vs. opportunities, 4) What we might overlook, 5) Questions before deciding. Realistic, not optimistic/pessimistic.
```
