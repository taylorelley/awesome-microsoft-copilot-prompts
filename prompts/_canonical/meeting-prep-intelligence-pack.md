## Context
Use this prompt when you need comprehensive preparation for an upcoming meeting including background context, stakeholder perspectives, and talking points.

## Prompt
```
Prepare me for my upcoming meeting with [PERSON/TEAM] about [TOPIC]. Based on recent emails, chats, and documents: 1) Background context and history, 2) Key issues or topics to discuss, 3) Their perspective and priorities, 4) My commitments or action items, 5) Open questions to address, 6) Potential concerns or objections, 7) Recommended talking points and approach, 8) Desired outcomes and success criteria. Format as a meeting prep brief.
```
