## Context
Use this prompt when you are launching something new and need to prepare a comprehensive FAQ for your audience.

## Prompt
```
Launching [product/service/initiative] for [audience]. Generate 10 FAQs: 1) Mix basic and advanced, 2) Cover benefits/costs/how-to/concerns, 3) Draft clear answers (2-3 sentences each), 4) Flag need for detail
```
