## Context
Use this prompt when a project or sprint has finished and you need to run a retrospective focused on learning and improvement.

## Prompt
```
Completed [project/sprint/initiative]. Retrospective: 1) What went well, 2) What didn't, 3) Why (root causes), 4) Do differently next, 5) Who owns improvements. Focus on learning, not blame.
```
