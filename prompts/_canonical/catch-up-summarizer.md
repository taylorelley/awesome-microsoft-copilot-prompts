## Context
Use this prompt when you missed a meeting and need a comprehensive catch-up summary covering decisions, action items, and follow-ups.

## Prompt
```
I missed the meeting on [TOPIC]. Provide a comprehensive catch-up including: 1) Meeting summary (key discussion points), 2) Decisions made and rationale, 3) Action items assigned (who/what/when), 4) Open questions or unresolved issues, 5) Next steps and timeline, 6) What I need to do as a result, 7) Key quotes or important statements, 8) Recommended follow-up actions for me. Make it thorough enough to feel like I attended.
```
