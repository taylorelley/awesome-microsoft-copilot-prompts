## Context
Use this prompt before agency sync meetings to compile deliverables, feedback, outstanding items, timeline discussions, and budget concerns from communications.

## Prompt
```
Search for all communications with [Agency Name] regarding [Project]. Compile:
- Deliverables discussed and their status
- Feedback provided on their work
- Outstanding items or revisions needed
- Timeline discussions
- Budget or scope concerns raised

I need this for our agency sync meeting.
```
