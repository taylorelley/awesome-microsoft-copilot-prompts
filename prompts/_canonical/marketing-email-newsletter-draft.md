## Context
Use this prompt to draft email newsletters with subject lines, preview text, content sections, CTAs, and personalization placeholders based on brand voice.

## Prompt
```
Draft an email newsletter about [Topic/Theme] for our [Audience Segment]. Include:
- Compelling subject line options (3)
- Preview text
- Main content sections
- CTAs
- Personalization placeholders

Based on our brand voice and previous newsletters. I will finalize in our email platform.
```
