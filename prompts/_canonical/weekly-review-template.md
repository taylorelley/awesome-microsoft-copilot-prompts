## Context
Use this prompt when you want to conduct a quick weekly review to reflect on wins and misses and plan the week ahead.

## Prompt
```
Weekly review for [day]. Include: 1) Last week (wins/misses/learnings), 2) This week (top 3), 3) Blockers needing attention, 4) Metrics check, 5) One improvement. Under 15 minutes.
```
