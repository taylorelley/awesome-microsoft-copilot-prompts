## Context
Use this prompt when you need to analyze communication patterns with stakeholders and get relationship management strategies.

## Prompt
```
Map communication volume and tone for each sender in the last 90 days. Categorize relationships as: 1) Supportive (advocates, helpers, collaborators), 2) Neutral (transactional, occasional), 3) Demanding (high-maintenance, urgent requests), 4) At-risk (declining engagement, tension, frustration). For each category, provide: Communication frequency, Average response time, Tone trends, Recommended relationship management strategy.
```
