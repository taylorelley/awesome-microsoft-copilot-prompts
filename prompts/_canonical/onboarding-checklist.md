## Context
Use this prompt when you need to design an onboarding plan that covers first-day, first-week, and first-month activities.

## Prompt
```
Onboarding for [role/team/project]. Timeline: [first day/week/month]. Include: 1) Day 1, 2) Week 1, 3) First month, 4) Who to meet/why, 5) Resources/access, 6) Success measured how
```
