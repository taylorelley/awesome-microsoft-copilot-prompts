## Context
Use this prompt when you need to predict your upcoming workload based on email patterns and get pacing strategy recommendations.

## Prompt
```
Based on the last 60 days of emails, predict my workload for next month including: 1) Expected email volume by week, 2) Likely deadlines and time commitments, 3) Task clusters (related work items), 4) High-pressure weeks (crunch times), 5) Meetings and collaborative work load, 6) Personal bandwidth vs. expected demand. Suggest pacing strategy and workflow optimization recommendations to balance load.
```
