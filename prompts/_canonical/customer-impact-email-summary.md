## Context
Use this prompt when you need to identify impacts on customers or partners from an email thread and get suggestions to reduce perceived risk.

## Prompt
```
From the thread below, summarize any impacts on customers or external partners. Provide **3 suggestions** I can include in my reply to reduce perceived risk.
```
