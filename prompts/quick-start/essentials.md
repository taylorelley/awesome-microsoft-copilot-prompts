### 1. Pros & Cons List

**Use Case:** Evaluating options thoroughly

**Target Personas:** Manager, Professional, Executive, Analyst, Decision Maker

**Tags:** `Copilot`, `Decision Making`, `Analysis`, `Evaluation`, `Quick Start`

**Prompt:**

